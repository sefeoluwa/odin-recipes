French Toast

French toast is traditionally made with bread and some additional ingredients. It's tender, fluffy, and the recipe comes together quickly and easily.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 6 thick slices of bread
  • 1 tablespoon of unsalted buter, or more as required.
  • 1 tablespoon of honey.

Steps to cook

  1. Step 1: Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  2. Step 2: Lightly butter a griddle and heat over medium-high heat.
  3. Step 3: Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side. Serve hot.
